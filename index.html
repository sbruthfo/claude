Our Mission

Her Equity Collective is dedicated to breaking barriers in women’s healthcare by expanding access, amplifying underserved voices, and building healthier futures through education, advocacy, and equitable wellness initiatives.

Meet the Founder

Maddison Ruthford

Maddison Ruthford founded Her Equity Collective after over a decade working in public health and maternal wellness advocacy. Witnessing firsthand the inequities affecting women’s healthcare access inspired her to create a nonprofit centered on compassion, education, and systemic change.

Today, Maddison leads national partnerships focused on reproductive justice, culturally informed care, and community-based health equity programs.
